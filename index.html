A beautiful, web based, MySQL management app.

Fast & lightweight

Cabindb has been written from the ground up with a focus on speed, where older tools haven't aged well to make the most of modern web technologies.

Familiar but fresh

You'll feel at home using Cabindb right from the get go. We've made sure the interface is familiar to other popular tools with a fresh and well thought out feel.

PHP & MySQL

Cabindb is built on a lightweight PHP code-base with a fast javascript front-end powering all your interactions, helping you administer your db's quicker & easier than ever.

Download

Cabindb is an open source project, free to use (GNU licence).

Cabindb is in the very early stages of decelopment. Currently it's barely in alpha and therefore it is changing rapidly and lacking in many features. Use at your own risk.

Guide

Installation

  1. Download the souce from one of the above options.
  2. Place the 'cabin-db' directory into your webserver's www or equivelent folder.
  3. Browse to the install's directory and log in with your MySQL user credentials.
